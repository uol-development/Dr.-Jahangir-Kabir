About Dr. Jahangir Kabir

A life dedicated to helping people live healthier lives through lifestyle-focused healthcare, education, and public awareness.

Dr. Jahangir Kabir is a physician, educator, researcher, and one of Bangladesh's most recognized advocates of lifestyle-focused healthcare. For more than two decades he has worked to help people understand the connection between everyday choices and long-term wellbeing.

The Philosophy

Throughout his years of medical practice, he observed a recurring challenge: many individuals were receiving treatment for health concerns while continuing to struggle with the lifestyle factors influencing their overall wellbeing. This realization inspired years of study, education, and public awareness focused on healthier daily choices.

A Movement, Not Just A Practice

What began as a healthcare philosophy gradually evolved into the JK Lifestyle Ecosystem — a network of organizations where healthcare, nutrition, fitness, education, organic living, and community support work together toward a shared purpose.
