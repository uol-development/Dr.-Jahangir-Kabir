Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers to Common Health Questions

Who is Dr. Jahangir Kabir?
Muhammad Jahangir Kabir, known as Dr. Jahangir Kabir, is a Bangladeshi physician, lifestyle specialist and health influencer, and the founder and chairman of JK Lifestyle. He completed his MBBS at Chittagong Medical College in 2000 and later trained in ultrasonography, diabetology, asthma and COPD.
What are the five pillars of the JK Lifestyle?
A balanced diet of organic, natural foods; autophagy through regular or intermittent fasting; regular exercise; adequate sleep with an early-to-bed, early-to-rise routine; and mental peace. Together they strengthen the body's natural immunity.
What is autophagy and why does he recommend fasting?
Autophagy is the body's natural process of renewing its cells. Dr. Jahangir Kabir recognises its vital role in keeping cells healthy and strongly advocates regular or intermittent fasting as part of a natural, disease-free lifestyle.
Can lifestyle changes really reduce reliance on medication?
Dr. Jahangir Kabir teaches that, by addressing the root causes of disease through lifestyle, many people can recover and stop relying on medication. Always consult your own physician before changing any treatment, especially if you have a medical condition.
