Who is Dr. Jahangir Kabir?

A Physician Devoted To Everyday Health

MBBS, Chittagong Medical College (2000) · 25+ years in lifestyle medicine

Dr. Jahangir Kabir is a physician, educator, and researcher, and one of Bangladesh's most recognised advocates of lifestyle-focused healthcare. For more than two decades he has worked to help people understand the connection between everyday choices and long-term wellbeing.

Throughout his years of practice he observed a recurring challenge: people were being treated for health concerns while still struggling with the lifestyle factors shaping their overall health. That realisation became a mission — to make preventive, evidence-informed health knowledge accessible to everyone.

The Philosophy

Better Health Is Built, Not Prescribed

His approach rests on a simple idea: sustainable health comes from better daily choices — nutrition, movement, sleep, stress and habits — supported by, and never replacing, proper medical care. What began as a healthcare philosophy gradually grew into the JK Lifestyle Ecosystem, a network of organisations working toward a shared purpose.
