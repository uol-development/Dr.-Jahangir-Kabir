Who is Dr. Jahangir Kabir?

A Life Dedicated To
Helping People Live Healthier Lives

MBBS, Chittagong Medical College (2000) · 25+ years in lifestyle medicine

Dr. Jahangir Kabir is a physician, educator, researcher, and one of Bangladesh's most recognized advocates of lifestyle-focused healthcare.

Throughout his years of medical practice, he observed a recurring challenge. Many individuals were receiving treatment for health concerns while continuing to struggle with the lifestyle factors influencing their overall wellbeing.

This realization inspired years of study, education, and public awareness efforts focused on helping people better understand how healthier choices can influence long-term wellbeing.

What began as a healthcare philosophy gradually evolved into something much larger. A movement dedicated to helping people build healthier lives.
