Who is Dr. Jahangir Kabir?

A Physician On A Mission

MBBS, Chittagong Medical College (2000) · CCD, Diabetology (BIRDEM) · Diploma Modules in Asthma, Diabetes & COPD (England)

Muhammad Jahangir Kabir — known to millions as Dr. Jahangir Kabir — is a Bangladeshi physician, lifestyle specialist, and health influencer, and the Founder and Chairman of JK Lifestyle.

Through his platform JK Lifestyle, he has popularised the concept of achieving a disease-free and medicine-free life by changing one's lifestyle — explaining these ideas through the lens of medical science.

Maintaining these principles strengthens the body's natural immunity and helps people stay healthy. Even those who have fallen ill through years of unhealthy habits can recover and stop relying on medication by adopting these lifestyle practices.

What began in clinical practice grew into a movement — and, across Facebook and YouTube, into a community of nearly ten million followers learning to live healthier, more natural lives.

The Five Pillars

Built On Five
Everyday Pillars

Dr. Jahangir Kabir's health philosophy rests on five fundamental pillars of lifestyle — simple, natural habits that strengthen the body's immunity and help it heal on its own.

  • Balanced DietOrganic, natural foods — no processed or chemically-treated items
  • AutophagyRegular or intermittent fasting to renew healthy cells
  • Regular ExerciseWalking, workouts and strength training every day
  • Adequate SleepEarly to bed (9–10 PM) and early to rise
  • Mental PeaceInner calm, because mind and body are connected
